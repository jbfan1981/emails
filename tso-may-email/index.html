Thank a teacher at TeacherShoutOut.com
Announcing the Herff Jones Teacher Shout Out Award recipients.

Over the past few months, you’ve submitted Shout Outs, nominated, and voted for your favorite educators, giving them the opportunity to win valuable rewards.

We are happy to announce that the wait is over. The votes have been tallied and the ten educators who received the most votes have been determined. These gifted professionals will each share a $5,000 reward with their schools.

Visit TeacherShoutOut.com to see the list on the Herff Jones Teacher Shout Out home page.

The entire Herff Jones Team would like to thank you for your participation. Although the contest is over, we encourage you to return to TeacherShoutOut.com, submit a Shout Out at any time as a way to just say thanks, and read what others are saying about the educators who have made a difference in their lives.
