You’ll find the latest advances in breast imaging technology at Cadence Health.
3D mammography is proven to:*
• Detect smaller breast cancers earlier
• Provide greater accuracy in pinpointing tumor size and location
• Reduce the number of patients who require follow-up testing
Both CDH and Delnor offer a complete range of breast care services, and 3D mammography screening for women who have been identified as having dense breast tissue or who are at a higher risk for developing breast cancer.
To see if 3D mammography is right for you, discuss this screening with your doctor. To schedule your screening at Cadence Health, call 630.933.5000. TTY for the hearing impaired 630.933.4833.
A doctor's written order is required for the screening.

*Typically 3D mammography is performed on women who have been previously identified as having dense breast tissue, those who have a family history of breast cancer or who may be at a higher risk for developing the disease.
